An algorithm is required to simulate a simple RPG battle where the player repeatedly attacks a monster with 50 HP until its health drops to 0 or below.

Write an algorithm to:

  • set the monster's starting health to 50
  • prompt the player to enter the damage dealt in that turn and subtract it from the health
  • repeatedly take damage inputs and subtract them from the health until the monster's health drops to 0 or below
  • output a victory message when the monster is defeated

Let's break this exam question down! We will look at how to code each bullet point step-by-step. At the end, you will put it all together in a real Python interpreter.

1

"set the monster's starting health to 50"

To do this, we create a Variable. Think of a variable as a box with a label on it. We use it to store information that might change later. Let's create a box called monster_hp and put the number 50 inside it.

monster_hp = 50

2

"prompt the player... and subtract it"

To attack, we need to ask the player how much damage they want to do. We use input() to ask a question, and int() to make sure their answer is treated as a math number (an integer). Then, we subtract that damage from the monster's HP!

damage = int(input("Enter damage dealt: "))
monster_hp = monster_hp - damage
